Mushroom Curry
Ingredients:
250g mushrooms
1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
Salt to taste
1/2 cup grated coconut
4-5 red chilies
4-5 garlic cloves
A handful of curry leaves
1 tablespoon coriander seeds
1 teaspoon cumin seeds (jeera)
2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
1 large onion, chopped
Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)
Instructions:
Prepare the Mushrooms:

Boil or cook the mushrooms by adding some turmeric and salt to taste.
Make the Curry Paste:

Grind the grated coconut with red chilies, garlic, curry leaves, coriander seeds, and jeera. Keep it aside.
Cook the Onions:

Add ghee to a hot pan and add the chopped onion. Fry until golden brown.
Cook the Curry Base:

Add the prepared curry paste to the fried onions and fry it until the raw smell disappears.
Combine Ingredients:

Add the cooked mushrooms to the pan and boil them together.
Finish the Curry:

Add some chopped coriander leaves. Your mushroom curry is ready to serve.
